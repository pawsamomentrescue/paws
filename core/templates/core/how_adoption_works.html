How Adoption Works

Our goal is to match each animal with a safe, loving home. Here's how the process works.

  1. 1. View

    Browse adoptable pets and shortlist animals that fit your lifestyle, home, and experience.

  2. 2. Apply

    Submit an adoption application so our team can understand your home, schedule, and preferences.

  3. 3. Meet

    We arrange a meet-and-greet with the pet, answer questions, and help you prepare for adoption.
